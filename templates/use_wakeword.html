WakeWord detector

Instructions

  1. Choose your wakeword from the dropdown
  2. Click the "Submit" button
  3. Click the "Stream" button
  4. Give the broswer permission to use your microphone
  5. Start talking! When the app detects your wakeword, you'l see an indicator flash on the screen
If you'd like to create your own wakeword, go to the create wakeword page.
